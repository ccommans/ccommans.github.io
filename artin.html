Solutions to Artin's Algebra, 2nd ed.

This project is (planned to be) a complete, carefully typeset solution set to Michael Artin's Algebra (2nd Edition). The PDFs below correspond to each chapter with a hyperlinked table of contents. Feel free to reach out via email with corrections or suggestions. I am currently working on this project in my spare time, so please be patient as I continue to add more chapters, and in the meantime feel free to visit Brian Bi's solutions to selected exercises.
